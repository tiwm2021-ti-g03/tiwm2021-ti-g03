Who we are?

Established in 2021, the ForestSons Association is a non-profit organization composed of the directors of forestry agencies in Portugal. Their members manage and protect state and private forests, which encompass nearly 3.3 million hectares.

What we do?

We are a team of volunteers that clean forests helping our environment and at the same time have fun socializing and making the world a better place. Each time we are getting bigger and stronger and more trash is removed from our beautiful forests

Our history

We start this team with only 15 volunteers last year in 2021 and just cleaned one forest and by now we are more than a thousand volunteers and we already cleaned 40% of the Portuguese forests.

Why this name and logo?

Like the name says we are the sons of the forests because we have big respect for them and we know the importance that they have to our environment and for us. The logo is simplistic and just represents an imaginary forest with our name above.
